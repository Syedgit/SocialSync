## FAQ Content

### Common Questions

**Q: What platforms does SocialSync support?**
A: SocialSync supports Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, and YouTube. We're constantly adding more platforms based on user requests.

**Q: Is there a free version?**
A: Yes! Our free tier includes 3 social accounts and 10 scheduled posts per month. Perfect for getting started.

**Q: Can I use SocialSync on my phone?**
A: Absolutely! SocialSync is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works like a native app on iOS, Android, and desktop. You can even install it on your home screen.

**Q: How is SocialSync different from Buffer/Hootsuite?**
A: SocialSync is built mobile-first with a modern, intuitive interface. We focus on simplicity and speed, making it perfect for small businesses and creators who don't need enterprise complexity.

**Q: Do you offer team collaboration?**
A: Team features are coming soon! For now, SocialSync is perfect for solo users and small teams. Sign up to be notified when team features launch.

**Q: Can I schedule posts in advance?**
A: Yes! Schedule posts days, weeks, or months in advance. Our calendar view makes it easy to plan your content strategy.

**Q: What analytics do you provide?**
A: We provide post performance metrics, engagement tracking, platform distribution, and growth insights. Advanced analytics are available in our Pro plan.
